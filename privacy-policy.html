Information Collection and Tracking Technologies

The technologies we use for automatic information collection may include:

  • Web Beacons. Pages of the App may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that permit the Company, for example, to count users who have visited those pages and for other related app statistics (for example, recording the popularity of certain app content and verifying system and server integrity). Web beacons may also be used to ensure appropriate user access to online support documentation for the App or to provide introductory (i.e., walkthrough) information for new features or functionality of the App.
